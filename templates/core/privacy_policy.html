Privacy Policy

Introduction

At WaveLift, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our smartwatches and headphones. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at support@wavelift.com.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you place an order, create an account, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and shipping address.

  • Payment Information: We collect payment details (e.g., credit/debit card numbers) through secure payment gateways but do not store this information on our servers.

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, products viewed, and IP address, using cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders for smartwatches and headphones, including shipping and customer support.

  • Personalization: To tailor your shopping experience, such as recommending products or sending personalized offers.

  • Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional emails (you can opt out of marketing emails at any time).

  • Analytics: To analyze website usage and improve our services, using aggregated and anonymized data.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, shipping companies) who assist in delivering our services, under strict confidentiality agreements.

  • Legal Compliance: Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, such as remembering your cart items or analyzing site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal information. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to its processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@wavelift.com. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our team at support@wavelift.com or through our website’s contact form.

Thank you for trusting WaveLift with your personal information.
